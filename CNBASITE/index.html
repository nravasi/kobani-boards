The Club

Founded on 12 October 1909 by employees of Banco de la Nación Argentina, CABNA has grown into a thriving athletic and social community based in Vicente López.

Today, CABNA offers more than 25 activities and sports — from football and basketball to judo, yoga, and artistic gymnastics. Our facilities welcome members of every age and skill level.

We are guided by the values that shaped us from day one: fair play, solidarity, and community.
